Lasagna

Home

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish, and a type of wide, flat pasta, featuring alternating layers of noodles, ragù (meat sauce), béchamel or ricotta cheese, and mozzarella.

It is known for its rich, hearty, and savory, melt-in-your-mouth flavor, often assembled in a casserole dish, baked until bubbling, and cut into squares for serving.

Ingredients

  • ground meat (beef/pork/sausage)
  • tomato products
  • onions
  • garlic
  • mozzarella
  • ricotta
  • parmesan
  • lasagna noodle

Steps:

  1. Meat Sauce: Brown 1 lb of ground beef/sausage with onions and garlic. Add marinara sauce (approx. 24–32 oz), simmer for 15–20 minutes, and season to taste.
  2. Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix 15 oz Ricotta cheese, 1 egg (optional, helps bind), 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, and parsley.
  3. Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente (usually 4–5 minutes), or use "no-boil" noodles for convenience.
  4. Topping: Shred 2–3 cups of mozzarella cheese.
